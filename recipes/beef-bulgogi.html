Beef Bulgogi

Description

Bulgogi, or Korean barbecue, literally means "fire meat." Thin slices of beef (or sometimes pork) are marinated in a sweet-savory sauce, then grilled to juicy and flavorful perfection.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Green Onions
  • Garlic
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Sesame Oil
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut Steak into flanks.
  2. Place flanks of steak in a bowl.
  3. Combine all ingrediants to make a marinating sauce.
  4. Pour marinade over the steak until it is fully covered.
  5. Cover bowl and let marinate for an hour.
  6. Set lightly oiled grill to high.
  7. Cook beef until charred and thoroughly cooked.
